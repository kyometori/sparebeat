Introduction of Categories

Category of a song is decided by how hard the difficulty "Hard" of the song is.
Here is a list of the difficulty range of a category:
  • Category Easy: 12-
  • Category Medium: 12 ~ 15
  • Category Hard: 15+
The category Boss Fight is an exception of the rules above. Maps in this category are made for challenging the limit of human.
The difficulties of maps are being adjusted often since we have no idea what it should be. Those numbers are always for reference only.

Anyway, have fun.
